Event Report

Global STEM Education Centre
Inaugurated at Christ (Deemed to be University)

A milestone for transnational academic cooperation between India and Australia, transforming STEM education across the Indo-Pacific.

Dec 12, 2025
Bangalore Central Campus

Bangalore, Dec. 12 – In a milestone for transnational academic cooperation, Christ (Deemed to be University), India, in collaboration with Australian Catholic University (ACU), inaugurated the Global STEM Education Centre on Friday at its Bangalore Central Campus. The initiative aims to transform STEM education across the Indo-Pacific through research, innovation, and inclusive teaching practices.

Centre’s Vision & Collaborative Scope

Teacher Development

Professional development for pre-service and in-service teachers to implement effective STEM strategies.

Research & AI

Collaborative research in artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and digital literacy.

Inclusivity

Focus on inclusion of underrepresented groups, especially girls and marginalised communities.

Partnerships

University–school–community partnerships aligned with NEP 2020.
