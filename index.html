Our Vision

Establishment of ACU-Christ University Global STEM Education Centre as a transnational hub for innovation and research collaboration — addressing systemic inequities and building workforce readiness through cross-disciplinary programs and sustainable transformation.

Our Mission

  • ✔ Hands-on, experiential STEM learning
  • ✔ Build teaching capability for in-service & pre-service teachers
  • ✔ Promote cross-disciplinary collaboration
  • ✔ Bridge academia & industry via mentorships
  • ✔ Focus on inclusivity — marginalised communities & girls
  • ✔ Upskill faculty through targeted training & certification

Objectives

  • ✔ Enhance STEM participation through integrated learning in science, technology, engineering, arts, and math.
  • ✔ Build capacity of in-service and preservice teachers to implement effective STEM strategies.
  • ✔ Create a self-sustaining centre integrating research, creativity, design, and digital literacy.
  • ✔ Foster partnerships between university, schools, and community for long-term engagement.
  • ✔ Academic Collaboration: Joint curriculum, research supervision, publications, and student exchange.
  • ✔ Capacity Building: Teacher training, mentorship, joint certifications, and impact reporting.
  • ✔ ACU-CU Collaboration: Co-designing governance, joint research projects, and shared resources.
